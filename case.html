Wellness App

Aura Theraphy

Most people experience the loss of a family member at a young age and don't know how to deal with the situation, while also getting frustrated if they have to resort to unsafe apps.

According to the results of the surveys and interviews, 75% stated that they had suffered the loss of a family member between the ages of 16 and 24. This has meant that some of them do not have the help of a health professional. Many of them receive the recommendation of a family member or friend to make an appointment with a therapist or psychologist, and thus heal or cope with their bad grief. However, most find applications that are not entirely safe or the profiles of professionals are not endorsed.

Aura Theraphy Wellness App

Problem Statement

Most people carry bad grief, and it is in the most mature stage when they usually go to a mental health professional.

However, when they start looking for one of them, they find countless criticisms about the lack of security and few professional certifications.

Into the Wellness App

Goals

Create a network of mental health professionals, through an application that contains their certificates and is also safe.

To do this, we will use the data anonymization requirements through the HIPPA certificate or the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act. In addition to this, the OTP verification of the users will be used when they register in it.
